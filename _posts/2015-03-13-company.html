I have been given a assignment where the requirement is to choose a company that needs an updated website. The company that I chose is called CTX Fitness it is a gym off Edgewater drive in orlando florida.

Click here to see the CTX fitness current website

I have several goals to update the website. They are listed as following

  1. Site Architecture - The organization of the sight really needs works. I plan to have 4 main links

    • Training
    • Blog
    • Shop
    • Contact

    The training links and the blog will have sub categories but i am not sure that they need to be full blown links but maybe just filters.

  2. Color optimization - When I say this I mean that I will use color to my advantage by making the website as usable as possible. I am using the Lifetime Fitness website as a tool and baseline to build upon. During the discover period the client really liked this website.
